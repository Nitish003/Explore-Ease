💡 Essential Tips

Smart Travel Tips

🗓
Book Early, Save Big

Book flights 6–8 weeks in advance for domestic and 3–6 months for international to get the best fares.

🧳
Pack Light & Right

Roll clothes instead of folding, use packing cubes, and always carry copies of important documents.

💊
Health Precautions

Check vaccination requirements, carry a basic first-aid kit, and get travel insurance before every trip.

💳
Money Matters

Carry multiple payment options, inform your bank before travelling, and keep some local cash handy.

📱
Stay Connected

Get a local SIM or an international roaming plan. Download offline maps before your trip.

🌐
Respect Local Culture

Research dress codes, local customs and etiquette. A few words in the local language goes a long way.

🛫
Airport Tips

Arrive 2–3 hours early for international flights. Keep liquids in clear bags and charge your devices before security.

📸
Capture Memories

Charge all devices the night before. Back up photos to cloud daily. Don't forget to also enjoy the moment!
