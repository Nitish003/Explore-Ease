❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

What does travel insurance cover? +
Our travel insurance covers medical emergencies, trip cancellation, baggage loss, flight delays, personal accidents, and more depending on the plan you choose.
Can I buy insurance after booking my trip? +
Yes. You can purchase travel insurance at any time before your trip departs. However, for the best coverage (especially pre-trip cancellation), it's recommended to buy immediately after booking.
Is COVID-19 covered under travel insurance? +
Yes, our Standard and Premium plans include COVID-19 related medical expenses and trip cancellation due to COVID-19 diagnosis.
How do I make a claim? +
Call our 24/7 helpline (+91 98765 43210) immediately in case of an emergency. For non-urgent claims, submit documents to claims@exploreeasy.in within 30 days of return.
Are pre-existing conditions covered? +
Pre-existing conditions are generally excluded. However, our Premium plan offers optional cover for declared and stable pre-existing conditions at an additional premium.
