⚡ What To Do In An Emergency

Step-by-Step Action Plan

1
Stay Calm
Take a breath. Most situations have solutions. Panic makes it worse.
2
Call Our Helpline
+91 98765 43210 — 24/7 always reachable, anywhere in the world.
3
Contact Local Authorities
Police, hospital, or embassy as appropriate. We'll guide you on who to call.
4
Document Everything
Take photos, get FIR copies, save all medical reports for insurance claims.
5
Notify Your Family
We can contact your family on your behalf if you're unable to reach them.
